// 01. overview

What is Matruka?

Matruka is an AI-powered smart farming platform built specifically for Indian farmers, bridging the gap between cutting-edge agricultural technology and rural accessibility.

The platform operates entirely in both English and Hindi, ensuring farmers who aren't comfortable in English can still access every feature. From real-time weather and crop recommendations to uploading soil profiles and detecting plant diseases through images — Matruka puts an agronomist in every farmer's pocket.

Beyond AI features, Matruka aggregates real mandi (market) prices, locates the nearest mandis, and surfaces relevant government agricultural schemes — data that is often fragmented across dozens of portals and completely inaccessible to most farmers.

Built as a solo full-stack project, every layer — UI design, backend architecture, AI integrations, API aggregation, and deployment — was designed and shipped by a single developer.

// 02. problem & solution

Why Matruka Exists

⚠️

The Problem

  • Indian farmers lack affordable access to expert agronomic guidance
  • Crop disease diagnosis requires visiting physical agriculture offices — slow and costly
  • Mandi prices are scattered across government portals with poor UX
  • Government schemes go unclaimed because farmers don't know they exist
  • Most agri-tech tools are English-only, excluding Hindi-speaking rural populations
  • Soil analysis data sits in labs — farmers can't act on it digitally
🌱

The Matruka Solution

  • AI-driven crop recommendations based on real-time weather and soil data
  • Photo-based crop disease detection — point, shoot, get a diagnosis in seconds
  • Unified mandi price dashboard — all market data in one clean interface
  • Curated government schemes feed with eligibility filtering
  • Full bilingual support — seamlessly switch between English and Hindi
  • Soil profile upload and digital analysis with actionable crop suggestions

What It Does

🌾

AI Crop Recommendations

Enter your location and the platform fetches live weather, cross-references soil conditions, and recommends the optimal crops for the current season using AI models.

AI · Open Weather API · Soil Data
🔬

Soil Profile Upload

Farmers can photograph or upload their soil test documents. The system parses and analyzes the profile to suggest suitable crops and fertilizer strategies.

Image Processing · AI Analysis
🦠

Crop Disease Detection

Upload a photo of a diseased crop leaf. The AI model identifies the disease, confidence level, and recommended treatment — all within seconds.

Computer Vision · ML Model
📋

Government Schemes

A curated, regularly updated database of Central and State government agricultural schemes — surfaced based on the farmer's state and crop type.

Data Aggregation · Official Govt Sites
📈

Live Mandi Prices

Real-time commodity prices from mandis across India, aggregated from government data sources. See price trends and find the best market for your produce.

Live Data · Commodity Online
📍

Mandi Near Me

Geolocation-powered nearest mandi finder — shows distance, current prices, and operating hours so farmers can plan their market visits efficiently.

Geolocation · Google Map

// 07. challenges & learnings

What I Solved

01

Crop Advice

No personalized crop advice without an agronomist Farmers in rural India can't afford or access agricultural experts.

Solution

Matruka uses Gemini 2.5 Flash to analyze their location's real-time weather and give crop-specific recommendations instantly — no expert needed.

02

Government Schemes

Government schemes go completely unclaimed Billions in agricultural subsidies go unused every year because farmers don't know they exist.

Solution

Matruka surfaces official government scheme links directly — no middleman, no confusion, no missed benefits.

03

Mandi Prices

Mandi prices are a guessing game. Farmers often sell at a loss because they don't know what the same crop is fetching at a market 30km away.

Solution

Commodity Online's real-time price feed shows live rates across markets so farmers can negotiate from a position of knowledge.

04

Agri-Tech Tools

Agri-tech tools are English-only. Most farming apps assume English literacy, cutting out the majority of India's farming population.

Solution

Matruka is fully bilingual — every feature, every label works in Hindi.
